Paste your Google Bookmarks export here !

Intro

Small helper to migrate your Google Bookmarks to TagPacker.

Warning / Disclaimer / Get out of jail free card

WARNING: This is just something I knocked up to solve a problem I was facing. Use at your own risk. If you lose any data, your're on your own I'm afraid. Keep a copy of your exported bookmarks, in addition to this file.

With all that said, this should be pretty straight-forward :-)

Analyse

You've already copied and pasted this bit of code into your export file from Google Bookmarks, yeah?

When prompted, save the CSV file somewhere convenient.

Review

Okay, we've now got a CSV file with thousands of rows. Load the CSV file into Excel or Google Sheets or whatever (it's a tab delimited file, you may be best opening a fresh worksheet and pasting the file contents in).

In the spreadsheet you'll see a Delete column. Enter anything in this column and that link won't be imported later. You can just delete the row if you'd prefer.

You can also order by the Date field and maybe just delete any thing over 5 years old? Your choice.

Upload Reviewed Spreadsheet

Once you've completed your review, copy the content to the clipboard and paste into the Reviewed Bookmarks textbox below:

And click the button below.

The script will then generate multiple bookmark files so you can safetly upload in batches rather than one large file.
